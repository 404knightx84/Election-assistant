Project Description
Election Assistant – India
A digital guide designed to help Indian citizens understand and navigate the voting process with confidence. This assistant provides clear, step-by-step information on how to register as a voter, locate polling booths, understand required documents, and cast a vote correctly — empowering first-time voters and reminding seasoned voters of key procedures and updates each election cycle.
Purpose
Many citizens, especially first-time voters, young people, and those in remote areas, find the voting process confusing or are unsure of their rights and responsibilities. This assistant bridges that gap by offering accurate, accessible, and easy-to-understand guidance — entirely independent of any political party or candidate.
Key Features

Voter Registration Guidance – How to check/register on the electoral roll via the National Voters' Service Portal (NVSP) or Voter Helpline app
Eligibility Check – Age, citizenship, and residency requirements to vote in India
Required Documents – List of accepted ID/EPIC card and alternate photo IDs accepted at polling booths
Polling Booth Locator – Guidance on finding your assigned booth and constituency
Voting Process Walkthrough – Step-by-step explanation of what happens inside a polling station, including EVM and VVPAT usage
Important Dates & Deadlines – Reminders for registration cutoffs and election dates
FAQs – Common doubts (e.g., NOTA option, voting if not in hometown, postal ballots, PwD/senior citizen facilities)
